About

This project demonstrates responsive, accessible front-end skills using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

About Us

Riverbend Trails was created to celebrate the natural beauty and recreational opportunities of the Riverbend area. Our mission is to make it easy for locals and visitors to discover hiking and biking trails that fit their interests and needs.

We are committed to promoting outdoor exploration, healthy living, and environmental stewardship. By providing accurate trail information, engaging features, and a user-friendly design, we aim to encourage families, adventurers, and nature lovers to connect with the outdoors.

Objectives

  • Provide clear and accessible information about local trails, including distance, difficulty, and features.
  • Encourage safe, enjoyable, and sustainable use of hiking and biking trails.
  • Promote outdoor recreation as a way to support community health and well-being.
  • Create a platform that demonstrates modern web development principles, including responsive design, accessibility, and dynamic content.
  • Offer visitors ways to connect with us for feedback, questions, or community events related to Riverbend Trails.

Project Requirements Checklist

  • 3 pages + form action page
  • Fetch local JSON, dynamic items (15+), template literals, ES Modules
  • Modal dialog, localStorage, responsive nav
